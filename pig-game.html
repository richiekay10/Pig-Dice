Welcome to "Pig Dice Game" Info Center

The objective of Pig Dice Game is to score 60 points or more to the winning points before the other opponents does.

Game Rules:

Each turn a player gets to roll the dice. If a player happens to roll a 1 on any of their dice, they won't earn any points for that turn and the other player gets their turn. On getting 2-6 on the dice, the player's sum is being added to their current score, until the player decides to save this current score by clicking on the Hold button. If a player saves their current score, the other player gets their turn.

Enjoy!
